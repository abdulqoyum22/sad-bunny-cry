SAD BUNNY ($CRY) NARRATIVE

The Sad Bunny (CRY) token is based on a viral video of Bad Bunny crying after winning a Grammy, which people started using as a reaction meme across social media. The project uses that “sad/crying bunny” meme as its main theme. It’s promoted through on-chain updates, tracking posts on X/Twitter, and tying into big pop-culture moments (like the Super Bowl) to stay relevant in conversations. The idea is to grow the token through meme culture and social media trends, while developers can use creator fees to support things like buybacks, burns, and other token activities.
